Absolute - 

cm - Centimeters
mm - Millimeters
in - Inches
px - Pixels(1px = 1/96th of 1in)
pt - Points(1pt = 1/72 of 1 in)
pc - Picas picas(1pc = 12 pt)

Relative - 

%  - To parent element
em - To the font-size of parent element
rem - To font size of root element
vw - To 1% of viewport width
vh - To 1% of viewport height
